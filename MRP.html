What is an MRP?

What Is a Military Relocation Professional?

A Military Relocation Professional is a REALTOR® who has completed specialized training through the National Association of REALTORS® to understand the unique needs, timetables, and earned benefits of military service members and their families.

When your PCS orders arrive, the clock starts immediately. You may have 30, 60, or 90 days to find housing in an unfamiliar city — and an MRP has the tools, process, and urgency to make that happen without sacrificing your best interests or your benefits.

VA Loan Basics — What Every Military Buyer Should Know

The VA Home Loan program has helped more than 24 million veterans purchase homes. It remains one of the most powerful home buying tools available — and one of the most underutilized.

  • No down payment required for eligible borrowers — the biggest barrier to homeownership, eliminated.
  • No private mortgage insurance (PMI) — saving hundreds per month vs. FHA or conventional loans.
  • Competitive interest rates — typically lower than conventional mortgage rates.
  • Funding fee waivers — veterans with service-connected disabilities may be exempt.
  • As of August 2024, VA buyers can pay for buyer agent representation — full professional advocacy is available to you.
  • Assumable loans — VA loans can be assumed by a future buyer, a significant selling advantage.
