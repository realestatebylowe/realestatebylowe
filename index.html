About Bill

Philadelphia Real Estate Agent & Buyer Specialist

Bill Lowe is a Philadelphia-based real estate agent with Keller Williams Real Estate, specializing in helping first-time home buyers and real estate investors across the Philadelphia metro area. As an Accredited Buyer's Representative (ABR®), Bill provides expert guidance throughout the home buying process, from pre-approval to closing.

Serving Philadelphia, Bucks County, Montgomery County, Delaware County, and Chester County, Bill focuses on helping clients identify value, structure competitive offers, and make smart real estate decisions in today's market. His approach combines local market knowledge with a strong background in business, sales, and investment strategy.
