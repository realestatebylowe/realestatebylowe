Homes for Sale in Fishtown Philadelphia

Fishtown is one of Philadelphia most in-demand neighborhoods — known for renovated rowhomes, a vibrant arts scene, and strong investment fundamentals.

Why Buy in Fishtown?

  • Walkable with restaurants, bars, and nightlife
  • Strong appreciation and resale demand
  • Close to Center City and public transit
  • Popular with first-time buyers and investors
  • Mix of rowhomes, new construction, and multi-family

Real Estate Market in Fishtown

Fishtown features renovated rowhomes, new construction condos, and multi-family investment properties. Prices typically range from $300,000 to $800,000+ depending on size, finishes, and proximity to the waterfront.

Investment Potential

Fishtown remains one of Philadelphia strongest appreciation corridors. Rental demand is high, development continues, and resale velocity is among the fastest in the city.
